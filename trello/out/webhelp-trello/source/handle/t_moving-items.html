Moving items

  • Card:
    • Use drag & drop to move a card on the board.
    • To move a card between boards, open the card and under Actions click Move. Select the target board, the list and position to move the card to and click Move.
  • List:
    • Use drag & drop to move a list on the board.
    • To move a list between boards, open the list menu and click Move List. Select the target board and position to move the list to and click Move.
  • Checklist item:
    • Use drag & drop to move an item up or down within or between checklists on a card.
    • To convert an item into a card, click the item, then click Convert to Card. The new card is placed on the bottom of the same list.
