Subscribing

You see an eye-icon on the cards, lists and boards you are monitoring and you receive email notifications every time something changes on that item.

If you are also using the Trello App, you can see app notifications and badges on your mobile devices.

  • Cards: If you want to monitor the activity on a card, open the card and click Subscribe.
    If you are assigned to a card, you are automatically subscribed to it.
  • Lists: If you want to subscribe to a certain list on a board, click the arrow on the list title bar, then click Subscribe.
  • Boards: If you want to monitor a board, click Show Menu > Menu > Subscribe.

When you open Trello in the browser or in the app, the icons show if there are new notifications since you last closed Trello.

  • browser icon
  • Trello banner icon next to your user name
  • card icon
