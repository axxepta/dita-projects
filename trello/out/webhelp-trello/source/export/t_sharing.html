Sharing Trello contents

  • In order to view the shared content (boards or cards), the recipient has to be a member of the organization, if the board is org-visible, or to be a member of the respective board, if the board is private.
  • In order to contribute to shared content, the recipient has to be a member of the respective board.
  • Card: To share a link to a card, click Share and more, copy the URL under Link to this card and send it.
  • Board: To share a link to a board, click Show Menu > Menu > Share, Print, and Export, copy the URL under Link to this board and send it.
