Glossary

Trello Gold

Trello Gold enables additional features for individual users.

  • File uploads up to 250 MB
  • Custom backgrounds for boards
  • Stickers for cards
  • Emojis for cards

Costs: 45$ per year, or 5$ per month, or up to 12 months free when referring friends.

Trello Business Class

  • Google Apps integration
  • Admin privileges
  • Board creation permissions
  • Restrict organization invites by domain or email address
  • Board invitation permissions
  • Deactivate organization members
  • Observers on boards
  • View organization members activity, boards, and cards
  • Saved search
  • features for organization members
  • Bulk data export & Export history
  • CSV exports for individual boards
  • Billing information & Invoices

Business

Trello Enterprise

Trello Enterprise enables additional features for companies with at least 100 users. Apart from and Business Class, it adds the enterprise features:

  • priority email
  • phone support
  • dedicated contact person
  • privacy and security
  • simplified billing
