Shortcuts and tips

Press ? on your keyboard to access the shortcuts list in Trello.

Examples of shortcuts:

  • b - open Boards menu
  • c - archive card
  • d - open due date picker
  • e - quick edit card
  • f - filter cards
  • l+<number> - set label
  • m - assign members
  • n - create new card
  • q - cards assigned to me
  • s - subscribe to card
  • Space - assign self
  • t - edit card title
  • v - vote
  • w - board sidebar
  • x - clear all filters
  • ? - shortcuts list
  • @ - autocomplete usernames
  • # - autocomplete labels
  • ^ - autocomplete new card position
  • < > - move card to other list, left or right

The shortcuts list online: Trello shortcuts.

More tips:

  • Double-click anywhere on the board, to add a list.
  • Click and hold on the board background, to drag the board horizontally.
  • Mark boards as favorites (with a star) and drag them to re-order.
  • Submit multiple cards to a list, from spreadsheets or text lines.
  • Drag & drop attachments to cards.
  • Submit multiple checklist items to a checklist, from spreadsheets or text lines.

Taco will also point you to new features and tips.
