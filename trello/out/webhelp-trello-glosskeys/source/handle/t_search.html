Searching cards across boards

Only Trello for Web supports searching across boards. The Trello mobile apps currently search on the active window.

Use the search field on the Trello menu bar to search for items across all boards.

  1. Type the search terms in the search field.

    The search results are listed in a separate window, grouped by cards, boards and organizations.



  2. Click the item you were looking for, or refine your search if needed. Advanced search operators:
    • @username or member:<username> - cards assigned to a member.
    • #label-tag or label:<label-tag> - cards with a certain label.
    • board:<text-or-id> - cards on a certain board.
    • due:day, due:week, due:month, due:<number< and due:overdue - cards due in a certain period.
    • created:day, created:week, created:month, created:<number> - cards created in a certain period.
    • edited:day, edited:week, edited:month, edited:<number> - cards edited in a certain period.
    • description:<term>, checklist:<term>, comment:<term>, and name:<term>
    • is:open or is:archived - open or archived cards (by default, both are shown).
    • is:starred - only cards on starred (favorite) boards.
    • has:attachments - cards with attachments. Similarly for: description, cover, members, stickers.
  3. If you need to repeat the same search query, you can use the option Save this Search.
