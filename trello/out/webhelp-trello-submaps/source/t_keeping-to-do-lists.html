Keeping to-do lists

Keep track of your chores and errands, of reading lists, of travel arrangements, etc. on To Do boards.



(source: https://trello.com/b/keLKBhex/personal-to-do-list)

  • Set the list to Private, if you want to work on it alone and hide it even from your groups.

  • Add board members (family, friends, students or colleagues), assign them tasks and collaborate on an Organization list.

  • Let the community participate, by keeping a Public list.
