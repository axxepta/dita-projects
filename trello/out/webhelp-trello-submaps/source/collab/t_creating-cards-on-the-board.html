Creating cards on the board

The lists on a board contain cards, on which you write the content, like for example ideas, tasks, etc.

  1. On the bottom of the list, click Add a card, type the card title in the text field and press Add.
    You can rearrange the cards by drag & drop, even between lists.
  2. By clicking a card, you can edit its content.
